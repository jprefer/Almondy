Almondy Inn Policies

OUR ROOM RATES
  • Room Rates are quoted per night based on single or double occupancy and are subject to 13% Tax.
  • Weekend rates apply Friday and Saturday night as well as Holiday/Festival Sundays.
  • Reduced mid-week rates offered Sunday through Thursday night. All rates subject to change without notice.
MINIMUM STAYS
  • A 2-Night minimum stay is required during all weekends throughout the year.
  • A 3-Night minimum is required during Holidays and Special Event/Festival weekends.
  • No minimum stay required mid-week Sunday through Thursday night.
3 NIGHTS MINIMUM
  • 4TH OF JULY: July 3, 4, 5th
  • FOLK FEST: July 22, 23, 24th
  • JAZZ FEST: July 29,30,31st
  • LABOR DAY: September 2, 3, 4th
DEPOSITS
  • Reservations will be confirmed upon receipt of full payment for one-night stays, or 50% of total charges for multiple nights’ reservations.
  • Balance due upon arrival. Visa, Master Card, and American Express accepted.
CANCELS
  • Cancelations need to be made at least 14 days prior to arrival in order to receive a refund, less a $25 cancelation fee. Reservations cancelled within 14 days of arrival are non-refundable. If guests depart prior to their scheduled departure date, the entire stay will be charged to the credit card. No refund issued. Full penalty for No-Shows: If guests do not show up at the Inn on the day of arrival, the full stay will be charged to the credit card and no refunds will be issued.
CHILDREN
  • We regret that we are unable to accommodate children under the age of 13.
NO PETS
  • No pets please.
NO SMOKING
  • No smoking in the Inn.
CHECK IN
  • Check-in will be handled at Almondy Inn between 3:00 and 7:00 pm. We encourage guests to advise us of their estimated arrival time. For arrivals after 7 pm, prior arrangements to check-in have to be made.
CHECK OUT
  • Check-out time is 11:00 am.
PARKING
  • Off-street parking is available. One (1) car per room.
